=============================================================================
                      HOW TO USE AI FEATURES
=============================================================================

1. SELECT YOUR VEHICLE
   - Go to "Vehicle Select" page
   - Choose your car from the gallery
   - Vehicle persists across all pages

2. GET AI RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Navigate to "Parts Catalog"
   - Click "Get AI Recommendations" button
   - Select your goal: Daily / Track / Drag / Street
   - Adjust budget slider ($500 - $20,000)
   - Click "Generate AI Tuning Plan"
   - Review prioritized parts list with reasoning
   - Click "Add All to Cart" to simulate installation

3. VIEW RECOMMENDED BUILD PATH
   The AI shows:
   - Total projected power gain
   - Total torque gain
   - 0-60 mph estimate
   - Parts in priority order
   - Dependencies and warnings
   - Cost-benefit analysis

4. INSTALL PARTS
   - Click green "+" button on any part
   - Part added to your vehicle instantly
   - Stats update across all pages
   - New parts unlock further recommendations

5. USE AI TUNING COACH
   - Just drive with OBD data running
   - Coach analyzes throttle/RPM patterns
   - Tips appear in chat and as notifications
   - Get personalized shift points and warnings

6. ASK AI ASSISTANT
   - Click robot bubble (bottom-right)
   - Ask about: "What\'s my current RPM?", "Recommend parts for track", 
     "How much power will turbo add?", "Tuning advice for my M3?"
